About campusMarket

🎓 Built by Students, for Students

campusMarket is the official student marketplace for the HNDIT 2nd Semester group project. Our mission is to make campus commerce simple, safe, and accessible for every student.

🛡️

Safe & Trusted

Campus-only platform. All users are verified HNDIT students. Meet safely on campus.

💸

Completely Free

No listing fees, no commissions. Post unlimited ads for free throughout your studies.

⚡

Fast & Simple

Post an ad in under 2 minutes. Find what you need and connect with sellers instantly.
